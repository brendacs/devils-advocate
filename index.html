What is Devil’s Advocate?

DA is a project dedicated to helping you escape your political bubble. We hand-select one smart, thoughtful article, video, or podcast a week you’ll disagree with and deliver it to your inbox. We give you access to the best of the other side of the arguments dominating our political discourse. To see why we think this is so important, and how we do what we do, check out our manifesto here. Join a growing community of people interested in being better, more open-minded friends, thinkers, and citizens. Let us help you be your own devil’s advocate.

How did DA begin?

After the 2016 election, Jeffrey’s classmates at Berkeley began confiding in him that they didn’t know many people who voted and thought differently than themselves. Jeffrey wanted to use his moral philosophy background to expose his friends to smart people who disagreed with them, and DA was born.

How is DA funded?

DA is funded entirely via subscriptions. If you think what we do is valuable, consider becoming a subscriber via Patreon.
