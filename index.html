Devil’s Advocate is a project dedicated to helping you escape your political bubble. We hand-select one smart, thoughtful article, video, or podcast a week you’ll disagree with and deliver it to your inbox.

What something else?

We give you access to the best of the other side of the argument. We’re doing this because our civil discourse… well it’s hardly a shining example of Socratic dialogue these days. Research indicates partisan hatred across the developed world is skyrocketing.

What something something?

We’re choosing to live, work, and talk only with people who believe the same things we do - and we’re villainizing everyone else. The internet and social media are making it worse. We think that ought to change. We hope you do too.
